    <div style="display: flex; justify-content: space-between;">
        <div style="width: 48%;">
            <h2 style="font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 10px; color: #83a598;">Total Supply</h2>
            <p style="font-size: 1.1em;">42 Billion TBC tokens at genesis.</p>

            <h2 style="font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 10px; color: #83a598;">Transfer Taxation</h2>
            <p style="font-size: 1.1em;">Tax applied on transfers. Diminishes over time based on interactions. Part is burned, rest is saved for rewards.</p>

            <h2 style="font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 10px; color: #83a598;">Top Interactors</h2>
            <p style="font-size: 1.1em;">Users get points for interactions. More tokens transferred = more points. Top interactors get rewards from tax.</p>
        </div>

        <div style="width: 48%;">
            <h2 style="font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 10px; color: #83a598;">Whitelist Features</h2>
            <p style="font-size: 1.1em;">Whitelisted addresses exempt from tax. They don't count for top interactors. Only dev can modify whitelist.</p>

            <h2 style="font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 10px; color: #83a598;">Reward Distribution</h2>
            <p style="font-size: 1.1em;">Top 10 interactors get rewards. Non-whitelisted initiators receive additional incentives.</p>

            <h2 style="font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 10px; color: #83a598;">Deflationary Mechanism</h2>
            <p style="font-size: 1.1em;">50% of tax amount is burned. This reduces supply over time.</p>
        </div>
