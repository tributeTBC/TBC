<div class="tokenomics">
    <h1>Tribute Tokenomics</h1>
    
    <h2>Total Supply</h2>
    <p><span class="highlight">42 Billion TBC</span> tokens at genesis</p>

    <h2>Transfer Taxation</h2>
    <p>Tax is applied on transfers, which diminishes over time based on user interactions. A portion of the tax is burned and the rest is saved for rewards.</p>
    
    <h2>Top Interactors</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Users accumulate points based on their interactions.</li>
        <li>The more tokens they transfer, the more points they get.</li>
        <li>Top interactors receive a portion of the accumulated rewards from the tax.</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Whitelist Features</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Whitelisted addresses do not get taxed.</li>
        <li>Interactions from whitelisted addresses don't count towards top interactors.</li>
        <li>Only the developer can add/remove addresses to/from the whitelist.</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Reward Distribution</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Rewards are distributed amongst the top 10 interactors.</li>
        <li>If the transaction initiator is not whitelisted, they receive an additional incentive reward.</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Deflationary Mechanism</h2>
    <p>Half of the tax amount is burned, creating a deflationary effect, reducing the overall supply over time.</p>
