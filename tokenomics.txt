<div style="color: #333; line-height: 1.6;">
    <h1 style="font-size: 2.2em; margin-bottom: 20px; border-bottom: 3px solid #e74c3c; display: inline-block;">Tribute Tokenomics</h1>
    
    <h2 style="font-size: 1.6em; margin-top: 30px;">Total Supply</h2>
    <p style="font-size: 1.1em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="color: #e74c3c;">42 Billion TBC</span> tokens at genesis.</p>

    <h2 style="font-size: 1.6em; margin-top: 30px;">Transfer Taxation</h2>
    <p style="font-size: 1.1em; margin-bottom: 20px;">Tax is applied on transfers, which diminishes over time based on user interactions. A portion of the tax is burned, and the rest is saved for rewards.</p>

    <h2 style="font-size: 1.6em; margin-top: 30px;">Top Interactors</h2>
    <ul style="font-size: 1.1em; margin-bottom: 20px; list-style-type: none; padding-left: 0;">
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">Users accumulate points based on their interactions.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">The more tokens they transfer, the more points they get.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">Top interactors receive a portion of the accumulated rewards from the tax.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 style="font-size: 1.6em; margin-top: 30px;">Whitelist Features</h2>
    <ul style="font-size: 1.1em; margin-bottom: 20px; list-style-type: none; padding-left: 0;">
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">Whitelisted addresses do not get taxed.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">Interactions from whitelisted addresses don't count towards top interactors.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">Only the developer can add/remove addresses to/from the whitelist.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 style="font-size: 1.6em; margin-top: 30px;">Reward Distribution</h2>
    <ul style="font-size: 1.1em; margin-bottom: 20px; list-style-type: none; padding-left: 0;">
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">Rewards are distributed amongst the top 10 interactors.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">If the transaction initiator is not whitelisted, they receive an additional incentive reward.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 style="font-size: 1.6em; margin-top: 30px;">Deflationary Mechanism</h2>
    <p style="font-size: 1.1em; margin-bottom: 20px;">Half of the tax amount is burned, creating a deflationary effect and reducing the overall supply over time.</p>
</div>
